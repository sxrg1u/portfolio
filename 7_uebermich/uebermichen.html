02 HOBBIES & INTERESTS

Football

Football is more than a hobby — it's a core part of my life. I've been playing in clubs since I was four years old. My position: right winger — pace, dribbles and assists are my thing. I played for several years in Romania, then at FC Buchs, at LFV Liechtenstein, and I'm now back at FC Buchs.

Gym

Alongside football, the gym is my second sporting outlet. I train in a mixed style — strength, endurance, and everything in between — and try to get in up to five times a week. For me it's not just about aesthetics; it's about staying performant, building discipline, and clearing my head.

I train at FitnessUnited in Buchs SG, usually together with my friends.

Travel

Exploring the world is one of my greatest passions. New countries, unfamiliar cultures, and unexpected experiences give me inspiration and broaden my horizons like nothing else.

// My highlights so far

Japan

Japan feels like its own world. Temples in Kyoto, neon lights in Tokyo, incredible food — nowhere else have I experienced so many contrasts at once. A country that stays with you long after you leave.

Hawaii

Turquoise water, endless beaches and a relaxed atmosphere that is instantly contagious. Hawaii showed me how beautiful it is to truly slow down — every sunset there feels like a gift.

Barcelona

Three things make Barcelona special for me: the Camp Nou, the Sagrada Família, and FC Barça. This city combines architecture, emotion, and football like no other.

England

London is tradition and modernity in one city. Red double-decker buses, Big Ben, the Tower — and for a football fan, England is of course an absolute must-visit.

Safari in Africa Bucket List

My dream destination. To experience up close how lions, elephants and giraffes live in the wild — that's something no film can replace. Africa is right at the top of my list.
